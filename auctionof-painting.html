Auction of Painting for Charity

Started in 1987, Mother's Choice has since helped over 51,000 single girls, cared for 3,600 babies and assisted with 1,400 successful adoption cases. In 2014-2015, they accrued a reserve of $8.5million. This has now been invested to ensure services to those most vulnerable. I donated my canvas painting “No More Walls” 6 feet by 4 feet for their fundraising gala held on the 7th of May 2016 at the J.W. Marriot Hotel, Hong Kong. Very pleased that Mr Jamie Tadelis of S C Lowy won the auction of my painting. Thank you Mothers Choice for working on a good cause, glad to be a part of it.
